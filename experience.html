Cultural Unit Theatre consists of 8 departments which are Music, Crew, Skedsa, theatre, Dikir, Modelling, Seria Serangkai Tari and Eisart. I have joined Crew department since i am in semester 3. These are the crew members who work behind the scenes and are always the backbone of every performance of the cultural unit. Crew has many branches according to student interests, for example, students can join lighting, buying, fashion, props and audio. In this department, I have been appointed as the treasurer and I am responsible for taking care of the club's cash flow.
